GENERAL PRODUCT VISION:
The app should help users track personal finances in a very simple, friendly, intuitive, and motivating way. It must combine finance tracking with goal-setting, mini-game challenges, savings motivation, and clear visual progress. The product should feel modern, interactive, and accessible for users of all ages, including non-technical and older users.

CORE DIFFERENTIATION:
This is not just a budgeting app. It is a gamified personal finance coach. The app should make saving money and controlling expenses feel rewarding, clear, and engaging.
